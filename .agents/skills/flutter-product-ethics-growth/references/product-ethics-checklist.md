# Product Ethics Checklist

- Sensitive contexts avoid shame and pressure.
- Gamification is classified as fit, conditional, no fit, contraindicated, or open.
- Guardrails are explicit for conditional mechanics.
- CTA states the real action.
- Paywall language is transparent.
- Notifications are useful, calm, and respectful.
- Copy is localized for all supported locales.
- Metrics track user value, not vanity.
- Research is requested when market claims or current benchmarks matter.
